🍷 **Wine Quality Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

📌 **Project Overview**

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the Wine Quality Dataset to understand how different **physicochemical properties influence wine quality ratings**.

Wine quality is affected by several chemical characteristics such as:

- Acidity levels

- Alcohol content

- Sugar concentration

- Sulphates

- Density

Through **systematic data exploration and visualization**, this project identifies:

- Patterns in wine quality ratings

- Relationships between chemical properties

- Correlations between variables

- Differences between red and white wines

📊 Analysis Components

The project is divided into **three main analytical sections**:

1️⃣ **Red Wine Exploratory Data Analysis**

- Analysis of physicochemical properties specific to **red wines**

- Distribution analysis of wine quality ratings

- Identification of key correlations between features

2️⃣ **White Wine Exploratory Data Analysis**

- Exploration of **white wine chemical characteristics**  

- Distribution and variability of features

- Feature relationships affecting wine quality

3️⃣ **Red vs White Wine Comparative Analysis**

- Comparison between **red and white wine datasets**

- Identification of similarities and differences

- Analysis of features influencing quality across both datasets

📂 Dataset Information

The datasets used in this project come from the **Wine Quality dataset**, which contains results of **physicochemical tests conducted on wine samples**.

Each dataset includes the following features:

**Feature** |	**Description**
Fixed Acidity | Concentration of non-volatile acids
Volatile Acidity | Amount of acetic acid present in wine
Citric Acid |	Natural acid contributing to flavor
Residual Sugar	| Remaining sugar after fermentation
Chlorides	| Salt concentration
Free Sulfur Dioxide	| Free SO₂ level
Total Sulfur Dioxide	| Total SO₂ level
Density	| Density of wine
pH	| Acidity level
Sulphates	| Preservative contributing to microbial stability
Alcohol	| Alcohol percentage
Quality	| Wine quality rating (0–10 scale)

📌 The **Quality** variable represents the **sensory score assigned by wine experts**.

🔍 **Key Insights from the Analysis**

Some of the most important findings from the project include:

🍷 **Quality Distribution**

- Most wines fall within **quality ratings between 5 and 7**

- This suggests that **moderate-quality wines dominate the dataset**

🍷 **Alcohol and Wine Quality**

- **Alcohol content shows a strong positive relationship with wine quality**

- Wines with higher alcohol levels tend to receive **higher quality ratings**

🍷 **Volatile Acidity Impact**

- **Volatile acidity negatively affects wine quality**

- Higher acidity levels may reduce **perceived wine quality**

🍷 **Residual Sugar Differences**

- **White wines contain significantly higher residual sugar**

- This makes white wines **generally sweeter compared to red wines**

🍷 **Feature Correlations**

- Certain variables such as **density, acidity, and sulphates** show meaningful correlations with wine quality
